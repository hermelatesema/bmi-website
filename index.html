What BMI Measures

BMI compares your weight to your height and places you in a general category. It is a rough starting point, not the final word.

Limitations of BMI

BMI does not account for muscle, body composition, or many other factors. It should be balanced with how you feel and guidance from health professionals.

Focusing on Sustainable Habits

Instead of chasing quick changes, focus on small, consistent actions like gentle movement, rest, and nourishment.

About This App

Pacific Wellness BMI is designed as a calm, beginner-friendly starting point for people who are curious about their current fitness level. It combines a simple BMI calculator with clear explanations and gentle recommendations.

Over time, this app aims to grow into a space where you can track progress, explore accessible workout ideas, and build sustainable habits at your own pace.
